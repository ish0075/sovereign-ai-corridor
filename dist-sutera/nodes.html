Allanburg Node

Located at the intersection of major 500kV transmission infrastructure and the St. Lawrence River, Allanburg represents the eastern anchor of the Sovereign AI Corridor™. This 308-acre Provincial Crown land assembly features direct-tap transmission easements and immediate access to one of North America's most significant cooling water sources.

  • Land Status Provincial Crown
  • Total Acreage 308 acres
  • Grid Voltage 500kV direct-tap
  • Cooling Source St. Lawrence River
  • Distance to Water < 2km
  • Ready Timeline 12 months

Nanticoke Node

The Nanticoke node leverages 237 acres of Federal Crown land with direct access to Lake Erie's vast cooling capacity. This western anchor features 230kV grid interconnection and represents one of the most power-dense, cooling-rich sites in North America — capable of supporting PUE ratings below 1.1.

  • Land Status Federal Crown
  • Total Acreage 237 acres
  • Grid Voltage 230kV interconnection
  • Cooling Source Lake Erie
  • PUE Capability < 1.1
  • Water Depth 30+ meters
